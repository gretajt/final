About

This website was created for a Beginning Web Development course in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It is a collection of books that aim to educate about the history of the police and the prison-industrial complex, as well as provide visions for the abolition of those institutions. The site was created in response to the 2020 George Floyd uprisings, and the demands to de-fund and dismantle the police that have been amplified by those protests.

Most of the book titles listed are available online as e-books. Five of the titles are available for FREE in e-book format as of June 2020.
